About AJ Plus Security

Our Story

AJ Plus Security was founded in Dar es Salaam with a single conviction: that protection should be both deeply professional and genuinely intelligent. What began as a focused manned-guarding operation has grown into a full-spectrum security firm trusted by corporate clients, construction sites, institutions, and private estates across Tanzania.

We operate from our headquarters in Tegeta, Dar es Salaam, with teams deployed across multiple regions of the country. Every guard, handler, and controller we field is trained, vetted, and supervised under one standard — ours.

Our Mission

To deliver security that clients can stop thinking about, because we never stop thinking about it. We combine trained personnel, working dogs, and technology-driven monitoring into a single layer of defense around every asset we protect.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's benchmark for technology-first private security — the firm other security companies measure themselves against.

Integrity

Every guard is vetted, every report is honest, and every incident is disclosed — even the inconvenient ones. Trust is the product we sell.

Responsiveness

Threats do not wait for business hours, and neither do we. Our control room and patrol units operate around the clock, every day of the year.

Innovation

We pair physical presence with surveillance technology and data, because a guard with a radio and a guard with real-time intelligence are not the same guard.

WHY CLIENTS STAY

Built for accountability.

Clients don't renew with us because of a sales pitch. They renew because every shift is logged, every incident is escalated immediately, and every guard answers to a supervisor who answers to us.

Cash-in-Transit & Asset Escort

Moving cash or high-value assets is one of the few moments where a security gap cannot be absorbed after the fact. AJ Plus Cash-in-Transit & Asset Escort provides armed escort and secure logistics for cash movements, high-value goods, and assets that simply cannot afford a single point of failure.

Routes, timings, and handling procedures are planned in advance and varied deliberately, with every handover documented so you have a clear record of custody from origin to destination.

What's Included

Planned, Varied Routes

Cash and asset movements follow routes and timings that change to avoid predictable patterns.

Secure Transport & Armed Escort

Vehicles and escort teams are equipped and trained specifically for high-value transit.

Chain-of-Custody Documentation

Every handover is logged and signed, giving you a clear audit trail for every movement.

Who Relies On Cash-in-Transit & Asset Escort

  • Banks and financial institutions moving cash between branches and deposit points.
  • Retail chains with daily cash collections that need a secure, documented process.
  • Businesses relocating high-value equipment, documents, or inventory between sites.

Manned Guarding

When you need a security presence that is seen, respected, and ready to act, AJ Plus Manned Guarding puts trained, uniformed officers on the ground around the clock. Every guard fielded under the AJ Plus name has passed vetting, undergone tactical and customer-facing training, and reports through a clear supervisory chain — so you are never relying on a single individual's judgement alone.

Guards are matched to the site: corporate reception teams who represent your brand at the front desk, perimeter officers for warehouses and compounds, and static posts for residential estates. Every shift is logged through our digital Occurrence Book, giving you a verifiable record of what happened on your property and when.

What's Included

Vetted & Trained Officers

Every guard completes background screening and a structured training program before deployment to your site.

Digital Occurrence Book

Shift activity, visitor logs, and incidents are recorded electronically and available for your review at any time.

Supervisor Oversight

Field supervisors conduct unannounced site checks and are on call for immediate escalation if anything goes wrong.

Who Relies On Manned Guarding

  • Corporate offices that need a professional, visible presence at reception and on the perimeter.
  • Warehouses, factories, and compounds where unauthorized access has to be stopped at the gate.
  • Residential estates and gated communities where families expect consistent, accountable coverage.
  • Institutions — schools, religious centres, and NGOs — that need calm, trained officers rather than untrained watchmen.

Construction Site Security

A construction site is at its most exposed exactly when no one is supposed to be there — nights, weekends, and the gaps between contractor shifts. AJ Plus Construction Site Security keeps a trained presence on site through every one of those gaps, protecting cement, fittings, cabling, machinery, and partially completed structures from theft and vandalism.

We brief our guards on your site layout, active work zones, and contractor schedules so they know what should be there and what shouldn't. As the build progresses and access points change, coverage is adjusted with it — not left running on day-one instructions.

What's Included

Night & Off-Hours Coverage

Sites are most exposed when work stops for the day; our guards hold the line through the night and over weekends.

Equipment & Materials Watch

Cement, fittings, cabling, and machinery are tracked and logged so missing items are flagged immediately, not weeks later.

Site-Aware Deployment

Guards are briefed on active work zones, contractor schedules, and access points specific to your build.

Who Relies On Construction Site Security

  • Contractors and developers managing active build sites with valuable materials on the ground.
  • Property owners protecting a site between phases, when work has paused but assets remain.
  • Civil works and infrastructure projects spanning large, hard-to-fence perimeters.

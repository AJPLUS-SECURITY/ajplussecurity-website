CCTV & Remote Monitoring

Cameras alone only record what happened. AJ Plus CCTV & Remote Monitoring pairs HD camera installation with a 24/7 control room that actually watches your premises in real time — long after the cameras go up and the installer has left.

We design coverage around your site's actual blind spots, not a one-size layout, and give you remote access to view your cameras and retrieve footage whenever you need it.

What's Included

HD Camera Installation

We design and install camera coverage based on your site's blind spots and high-risk points.

24/7 Control Room Monitoring

Live feeds are watched in real time by our control room, not left to record and review later.

Remote Access & Footage Retrieval

View your cameras from your phone, and pull recorded footage whenever you need it.

Who Relies On CCTV & Remote Monitoring

  • Warehouses and retail outlets that need visual oversight beyond what guard reports can cover.
  • Residential estates and offices wanting a verifiable record of who came and went.
  • Clients who want technology working alongside — or instead of — a static guard.
